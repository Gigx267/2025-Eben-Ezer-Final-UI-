EBEN EZER AUXILIARY

Elevating Lives Through Community & Legacy

Mission: The Eben Ezer Auxiliary fuels transformative impact—bridging fundraising, historic preservation, and joy-filled initiatives to uplift residents. Since its inception, the Auxiliary has been the financial and relational backbone of Eben Ezer's thriving community.

Key Initiatives

Fall Festival
The Crown Jewel of Community Fundraising Held every fourth Saturday in September, this flagship event funds resident activities, outings, and special projects. A celebration of connection, with 100% of proceeds directly enriching lives.
Historic Cemetery
Honoring the Past, Stewarding the Future The sacred resting place of Jens Madsen, his wife, and several deaconesses. Our restoration projects preserve this important heritage while maintaining its serene beauty for generations.
Culinary Fundraisers
Flavors That Fuel Our Mission From burritos to green chili and traditional grebbel, our culinary sales combine community, culture, and care—each bite supporting resident programs.

The Eben Ezer Auxiliary meets quarterly and welcomes new members passionate about making a difference. Join us in creating moments that matter.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Volunteers create a positive environment for our residents and are a real asset to our community. Even though a volunteer's job may not be difficult, their efforts bring meaning and growth to the lives of our neighbors. Volunteers are essential to Eben Ezer's ministry.

Make an Impact

There are many jobs that volunteers can do ranging from answering the telephone to visiting someone who is lonely. Volunteers can take a scenic stroll with someone who is wheelchair bound or they can accompany residents on a shopping trip or to a special event.

Your efforts can make a big difference in the lives of so many. Please donate your talents and a little bit of effort to make someone who is feeling lonely and forgotten feel important again.

JUNIOR VOLUNTEERS

Junior volunteers join in bringing their help and talents to the Eben Ezer campus. These volunteers create a positive force that enriches the lives of our neighbors and in return, these young people receive a valuable experience that will instruct and deepen the meaning of their lives.

Our Junior Volunteer program offers meaningful opportunities for young people to engage with residents, develop empathy, and gain valuable life experience while making a real difference in our community.
