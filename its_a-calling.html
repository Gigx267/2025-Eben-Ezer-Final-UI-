A Higher Calling in Care

A career at Eben Ezer Lutheran Care Center is more than just a job. It's a calling.

From housekeeper to cook to nurse, everyone works together to improve the lives of the people who call Eben Ezer home. The Eben Ezer team is second to none! You can look forward to great benefits, career development, and being part of our over 100-year legacy of serving older adults.

Our team helps older adults and their family members navigate the difficulties that come with aging and accessing the right-fit services when they are needed. Our non-profit, mission driven team is what sets Eben Ezer apart from others. We truly believe that this is our calling!
