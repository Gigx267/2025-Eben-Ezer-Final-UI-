Memory Care

Occasionally specialized care in a secure environment is needed. This specialized care is available for neighbors with memory loss in two distinct care options.

Skilled Nursing – Sommerlyst Neighborhood

Individuals with Alzheimer’s disease and other forms of dementia have unique needs that may present special caregiving challenges. We provide aid and support for family members to help in dealing with the stressors associated with their caregiving challenges.

  • Private and semi-private rooms in a secure neighborhood
  • Activities tailored to all levels of cognition
  • Lodge-style dining and central living spaces

Memory Care Assisted Living – Evergreen Neighborhood

This new home is the only, one of its kind, in Northeast Colorado! The Evergreens Assisted Living offers the security of memory care in an assisted setting.

  • All private rooms with outdoor courtyard
  • Centralized gathering spaces
  • Designed for the unique needs of memory care

Ongoing Specialized Education

Our entire team receives specialized training in Re-framing Dementia through the Eden Alternative, ensuring the best care for those with memory impairments.
