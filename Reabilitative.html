Rehabilitative Care

Eben Ezer Lutheran Care Center offers comprehensive rehabilitation services including physical, occupational, and speech therapy. These services help restore life functions and alleviate pain for those affected by:

  • Stroke recovery
  • Injury rehabilitation
  • Chronic pain management
  • Neurological disorders
  • Musculoskeletal conditions

Physical Therapy

Reduces pain while improving mobility, strength, balance, and endurance through therapeutic exercise.

Occupational Therapy

Enhances physical functioning and self-sufficiency through therapeutic methods and environmental modifications.

Speech Therapy

Improves communication, swallowing, and cognitive skills following injury or illness.
