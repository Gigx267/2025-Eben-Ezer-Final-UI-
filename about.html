Eben Ezer has been a vital part of Morgan County for over 100 years. Our expert, dedicated team provides the quality care older adults need to stay happy and healthy.

Our Century of Care

For over 100 years, Eben Ezer has been a cornerstone of Morgan County, dedicated to creating an environment where life thrives. Rooted in faith and driven by a mission to empower older adults, we believe in fostering independence, health, and fulfillment.

Our Commitment

  • Holistic Care: Addressing physical, emotional, and spiritual needs
  • Rehabilitative Excellence: State-of-the-art therapy services
  • Community Connection: Vibrant, supportive environment
  • Regional Growth: Expanding to serve northeast Colorado

Guiding Principles at Eben Ezer

Our values define who we are and drive our commitment to quality care, decision making, and service excellence.

Well-Being

We support Identity, Growth, Spirituality, Choice, Security, Connectedness, Meaning, and Joy in all aspects of life.

Trust

We build relationships through integrity and reliability in all our interactions.

Stewardship

We responsibly manage time, talent, and resources to benefit our community.

Mission Focused

Guided by faith, dedicated to care

Eben Ezer is a faith-based, not for profit, mission-driven organization that focuses on senior living care and services.

Our Campus Services

On our campus, we provide independent housing apartments, assisted living, memory care, and skilled nursing care and services.

Community Support

We also provide electronic devices supporting individuals living in their homes, throughout Northeast Colorado.

"We are a progressive, faith-based community working to provide person-centered care and to support the well-being and growth of those we serve."

We are guided by our mission in the care we provide, the decisions we make, and the services we provide.
