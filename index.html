About Our Community

Redefining Senior Living Through Compassionate Care

For over a century, Eben Ezer has been a cornerstone of Morgan County, providing exceptional assisted living, skilled nursing, and memory care services.

Our mission-driven team of caregiving professionals combines clinical expertise with heartfelt compassion to create an environment where older adults thrive. Grounded in our faith-based values, we measure our success through the vibrant lives of our residents.

We champion every individual's right to a purposeful life filled with dignity, independence, and wellness. At Eben Ezer, we've created a community where quality care and personal freedom intersect to support whole-person wellbeing for residents, families, and caregivers alike.

Our Core Values

Guiding Principles at Eben Ezer

At Eben Ezer, our values are more than what we do, it’s who we are. They serve as a driving foundation for quality care, decision making, and service. We are committed to fostering an environment that prioritizes well-being, accountability, trust, reputation, and stewardship.

Well-Being:

We value wholeness by supporting Identity, Growth, Spirituality, Choice, Security, Connectedness, Meaning, and Joy. We strive to create a nurturing environment where all aspects of well-being are honored and cultivated.

Trust:

We value integrity and reliability in our relationships. Building trust through consistent and ethical behavior is fundamental to our interactions.

Stewardship:

We value the cultivation of time, talent, and treasure. We are committed to responsibly managing and utilizing our resources to benefit our community.

Long-Term Care Reimagined

The Eden Alternative Philosophy

We've transformed traditional long-term care through the Eden Alternative, developed by Harvard-educated geriatrician Dr. William Thomas. Our "A Place Called Home" model creates a vibrant environment focused on companionship, meaningful activities, and person-directed care.

  • Personal autonomy in daily routines
  • Customized meal choices and preparation
  • Flexible schedules aligned with personal preferences

Neighborhood Living Model

Our innovative neighborhood design creates intimate communities within our facility, each with shared living spaces and consistent care teams that foster deep relationships and familiarity.

Description Statement – Mission Focused

Eben Ezer is a faith-based, not for profit, mission-driven organization that focuses on senior living care and services. On our campus, we provide independent housing apartments, assisted living, memory care, and skilled nursing care and services. We also provide electronic devices supporting individuals living in their homes, throughout Northeast Colorado.

As our mission states, we are a progressive, faith-based community working to provide person-centered care and to support the well-being and growth of those we serve. We are guided by our mission in the care we provide, the decisions we make, and the services we provide.
