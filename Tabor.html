Independent Living

Tabor Apartments is a 30-unit, recently renovated, apartment building for low-income independent living. Twenty-three apartments are one-bedroom units and seven are studio apartments, some with a paneled wall between the living area and the bedroom. Rent is based on a HUD formula.

Monthly Rent Includes:

🏠

Spacious Living

Large living rooms with TV outlets and telephone jacks. Bedrooms fit queen-size beds.

🚿

Modern Bathrooms

Fully remodeled with walk-in showers, new vanities, and safety handles.

🍳

Upgraded Kitchens

New cabinets, countertops, full-size stove, fridge, and garbage disposal.

🧺

Convenient Amenities

Coin-operated laundry, game tables, air conditioning, and ample storage.

The HUD Formula

Rent is 30% of adjusted income after deductions for medical expenses and a standard $400 allowance. Includes utilities (heat, cooling, water, trash).

Eligibility: Seniors (62+) or disabled individuals with low income (Social Security, retirement accounts, etc.).
